The following are illegal characters in a post tile: "#%&{}\*/$'@+`|=".

The following are not illegal characters: "ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ" and "abcdefghijklmnopqrstuvwxyz" and "1234567890" and "_.\-~\?! "
